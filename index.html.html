Weather Seasons

Spring




Spring is the season of renewal.
It is characterized by blooming flowers, warming temperatures,
and longer daylight hours.
The world awakens from its winter slumber.
The world comes filled with color
And gives some cheer.



Summer




Summer is the season of sunshine and outdoor fun.
It brings warm temperatures,
clear skies, and opportunities for swimming,
and enjoying the beach.
Many people take vacations during this time.
